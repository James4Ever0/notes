# DMA card

FPGA cards can be flashed with PCILeech firmware and perform DMA actions. It is also possible with USB3380 (slow) and BMC devices.

Hardware:

- Screamer PCIe Squirrel
- HP iLO
- Immortal DMA Warrior
- Xilinx Alveo U50, U200, and U250
- Netcope P4
- Intel PAC D5005 
- CaptainDMA M2
- Screamer M2
