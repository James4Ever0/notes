# time lapse body camera

Wear elastic necklace for pets.

Place esp32 or rp2040 based dev board with lightweight battery pack.

Assemble into waterproof case.

Use wide range camera with broad view.

Capture photos every x minutes, then perform deep sleep.

Transfer data to computer using serial, mass storage.
