You can find many of the platforms mentioned here categorized for you:

🌐 General Open Source Hardware Platforms

· Hackaday.io (Global Community): A large social platform for hardware projects with detailed build logs and active discussions.
· Open Hardware Repository (OHWR) (CERN/Global): Geared towards scientific hardware and complex projects like the White Rabbit timing system.
· Open Hardware Portal (Antmicro/Global): Features photorealistic 3D models and advanced designs (like FPGA boards) created with open-source tools.
· Wikifactory (Global): Allows teams to collaboratively work on both hardware files and documentation together.
· eLinux.org (Global): A wiki that curates embedded Linux projects, ideal for Linux-based hardware systems.
· DFRobot Community (China/Global): A well-established platform with a wealth of projects, tutorials, and forums primarily for Arduino and Raspberry Pi.
· Seeed Studio Project Hub (China/Global): Hosts projects for modular IoT kits, sensors, and developer-focused robotics hardware.
· OSHWLab (China/Global): An international version and global counterpart to OSHWHub, sharing the same EasyEDA ecosystem.
· Gitee Hardware Zone (China): A major domestic code hosting platform (Git alternative) with a dedicated hardware zone for local RISC-V projects.
· The JieLi Open Source Platform (China): A platform supported by JieLi Technology focusing on Chinese microcontroller ecosystems.

🔌 Specialized Platforms for Key Areas

FPC & PCB Design:

· KiCad Libraries (Tools & Parts): Provides an extensive component library from CERN and tools for simulating flexible circuits (FPCs).
· Topola (Tools & Parts): An open-source autorouter capable of more advanced layouts than traditional tools.

Robotics:

· Open Robotics (Global Organization): Home to ROS/ROS2 (Robot Operating System) and the Gazebo simulator; the de facto standard for professional and research robotics.
· OpenBot Waffle (Global Project): A wheeled robot with comprehensive documentation and simulation support using the ROS2 framework.
· ROBA (Emerging Platforms): Positions itself as "the Hugging Face of robotics," offering a unified cloud workflow for simulation and deployment.
· PyRobot (Global Project): A lightweight Python framework designed to simplify robotics research, often paired with the LoCoBot platform.
· XLeRobot (Global Project): An open-source dual-arm mobile robot platform focused on keeping costs low.

📰 Academic & Community Resources

· Academic Journals: HardwareX and the Journal of Open Hardware provide peer-reviewed, fully documented projects, ensuring high levels of reliability and reproducibility.
· News & Inspiration: Hackaday is a leading source for the latest news in hardware hacking, offering a constant stream of inspiration.

You can use keywords like "FPC", "flexible", "ribbon", or "flat cable" in the community project searches to find relevant designs. Good luck with your search—feel free to ask if anything else comes up.
